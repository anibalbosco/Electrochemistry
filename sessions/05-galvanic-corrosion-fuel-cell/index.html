Session 5 — Galvanic Corrosion and Fuel Cell Demo

Session 5

Hook: Connect corrosion, batteries, and renewable hydrogen storage.

Learning outcomes

  • Build metal-pair galvanic cells and measure voltage
  • Explain galvanic corrosion and sacrificial protection
  • Compare electrolysis vs. fuel cell operation (energy direction)
  • Synthesize the week's experiments in a final review

Session plan (90 min)

Time Activity Reference
0–10 min Hook: rusty iron, galvanized nail, copper wire lecture.md
10–25 min Galvanic cells, corrosion, sacrificial anodes lecture.md
25–45 min Metal-pair cells — measure voltage experiment.md — Part A
45–55 min Corrosion comparisons experiment.md — Part B
55–70 min PEM fuel cell: electrolyzer then power LED/fan experiment.md — Part C
70–80 min Compare electrolysis vs. fuel cell lecture.md
80–90 min Final challenge questions lecture.md

Key reactions

Galvanic: Zn(s) → Zn²⁺ + 2e⁻

PEM anode:  H₂ → 2H⁺ + 2e⁻
PEM cathode: ½O₂ + 2H⁺ + 2e⁻ → H₂O
Overall: H₂ + ½O₂ → H₂O
